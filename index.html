WHATS THE THEORY?

How CrossyBot Works

Markets move in trends — some fast, some slow, some lasting minutes, others months.
These trends don’t appear at random; they form across all timeframes, from short-term swings to long-term cycles.

At the heart of trend trading is one of the most trusted tools in technical analysis — the moving average. Moving averages help cut through noise, reveal momentum shifts, and highlight the true direction of price over time.

Automations

Moving average crossovers aim to capture the core of a trend — not the exact top or bottom — creating a rule-based, logic-driven system.

Mode 1: Auto Trade Opening

Automatically opens trades on moving average crosses which align to the higher timeframe trend.
Selections can be based on top 1–50 market cap coin crosses - or your own manual selections.

Mode 2: Closing Assist Only (always on!)

Doesn’t open trades.
Just monitors your Kraken account and closes open trades (even ones placed manually) on profitable moving average crosses.

Dual timeframe confirmation: Higher timeframe to define trend, lower timeframe for precise entries and exits.

Trend filtering: Only trades in the higher timeframe trend direction to improve success rates.

Risk management: Customizable % stop loss applied to every trade.

Long & short support: Trades in either direction, adapting to all market conditions.

Custom strategy tuning: Timeframes and moving average settings are adjustable to fit any trading style.
